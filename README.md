# HVAC Proposal Automation Platform (POC)

## Overview

The HVAC Proposal Automation Platform is a Proof of Concept (POC) demonstrating how Generative AI, Agentic AI, and cloud-native architecture can be applied to automate and accelerate the commercial HVAC proposal and submittal process.

The platform is designed to assist sales engineers, estimators, and technical specialists in evaluating bid opportunities, identifying equipment matches, extracting project requirements, and generating proposal artifacts while maintaining human oversight and approval.

This project was developed as an architecture-first exercise focused on solving real-world business challenges within the commercial HVAC industry.

---

## Business Problem

Commercial HVAC equipment vendors often receive hundreds of bid invitations and Requests for Proposal (RFPs) each year.

The proposal process typically requires:

* Reviewing project plans and specifications
* Extracting equipment requirements
* Identifying qualified equipment options
* Evaluating customer relationships and historical performance
* Creating proposal documentation
* Coordinating multiple subject matter experts

These activities are highly manual, time-consuming, and difficult to scale.

---

## Solution Vision

The platform uses specialized AI agents collaborating under a supervisory workflow to automate key stages of the proposal lifecycle while keeping human experts in control of final decisions.

### Example Workflow

1. Bid Qualification Agent

   * Evaluates incoming bid invitations
   * Identifies project attributes
   * Recommends pursue / no-pursue decisions

2. Specification Analysis Agent

   * Extracts equipment requirements from plans and specifications
   * Identifies performance criteria and constraints

3. Equipment Matching Agent

   * Compares project requirements against manufacturer product catalogs
   * Recommends candidate equipment selections

4. Customer Intelligence Agent

   * Reviews historical customer interactions and prior opportunities
   * Provides contextual recommendations

5. Proposal Assembly Agent

   * Generates proposal packages and supporting documentation

6. Human Review

   * Sales engineers review and approve recommendations
   * Final proposal decisions remain under human control

## Architecture Highlights

### Agentic AI

The solution utilizes specialized AI agents that collaborate to complete complex business workflows while maintaining separation of responsibilities and traceability of decisions.

### Human-in-the-Loop Governance

Recommendations generated by AI agents are reviewed and approved by qualified sales engineers prior to customer delivery.

### Multi-Tenant SaaS

The platform is designed to support multiple organizations while maintaining strict tenant isolation and enterprise security controls.

### Knowledge-Driven Decisions

The solution incorporates customer history, equipment catalogs, project specifications, and organizational knowledge bases to improve recommendation quality.
